About Margot Robbie

Born: on 2 July 1990 in Dalby, Queensland

Parents: Doug Robbie, a former farm-owner and sugarcane tycoon, and Sarie Kessler, a physiotherapist.

Education: She was enrolled in a circus school by her mother, where she excelled in trapeze, in which she received a certificate at age eight. In high school, Robbie studied drama at Somerset College.

In high school, Robbie studied drama at Somerset College.] As a teenager, she worked three jobs simultaneously: she tended a bar, cleaned houses, and worked at Subway. After graduation, with a few commercials and independent thriller films on her résumé, Robbie relocated to Melbourne to begin acting professionally.

She is a proud member of Greenpeace.

In 2014, Robbie founded her own production company, LuckyChap Entertainment.

In 2016, she married Tom Ackerley in Byron Bay, New South Wales, Australia.
